Tim Shields

Tim Shields is the founder of Photography Academy, author of The Photo Cookbook, and creator of the Photography Transformation Four-Step System.

In 2018, Tim was awarded "Nature Photographer of the Year" by Master Photographers International and holds the designation of Master Photographer in Fine Art (MPI).

Tim specializes in ultra-high-resolution panoramic images. His passion for the outdoors takes him to remote hike-in locations where he packs in his photography and camping gear in search of unique landscape compositions and warm "golden hour" light. Tim believes that mountains, streams, and oceans are the world's most beautiful pieces of art, and his goal is to capture vibrant images that reflect the true beauty of the earth so that a brief moment of perfect light can be enjoyed for years to come.

Tim Shields has traveled throughout North America, Europe, and China in search of the perfect landscape image. He has won numerous photographic awards and lives in White Rock, Canada. He is an honors graduate from the BC Institute of Technology in Civil and Structural Engineering and holds a master of arts degree.

Print only

Our photographic prints are created using a specialty, large-format fine art printer designed to produce archival, museum-grade prints. They are true "chromogenic" prints: light-sensitive paper exposed in a darkroom using state-of-the-art lasers to produce the sharpest possible image. Click on the icons below to learn more or click here to explore further.

Our photographic prints are available in one of two finishes: glossy and lustre. Glossy prints have a rich, silky saturation and are great for environments where the lighting and reflections have been carefully thought through. Lustre prints have a bright, sparkle-like finish that diffuses reflections and are therefore more versatile for environments where the lighting and reflections are less under your control.

Your print comes with two certificates of authenticity hand-signed by the artist and the printmaker: one that you will affix to the finished piece and one that remains standalone. Additionally, your print's provenance is tracked and verified using state-of-the-art digital technology. Learn more.

Our acrylic glass displays are ready-to-hang works of art that contain one of our photographic prints sealed between flame-polished acrylic glass and an aluminum backing. limited editions

VAST photos are truly one-of-a-kind artworks due to the unparalleled resolutions they have, the groundbreaking artists who create them, and the acclaim they receive. For many of our photos, we have further amplified that uniqueness by limiting the number of physical reproductions that we will create of the photo. These limited editions are cherished by their owners as rare works of art whose value can stand the test of time.

Each piece we create in a limited edition is assigned a unique serial number and recorded in our edition registry. Its provenance is then tracked and verified using state-of-the-art digital technology. Click here to learn more about our authentication process.

All of our limited editions come with:

  • The artist signature on the rear of the piece
  • A certificate of authenticity affixed to the rear of the piece by a security hologram imprinted with the photo's serial number
  • A standalone certificate of authenticity to be kept separate from the piece that can be used to demonstrate proof of ownership

NOTE: all editions are specific to a print type/medium. For example, a photo can have an edition for photographic prints and a separate edition for canvas prints. Additionally, some photos have editions that are specific to sizes. For example, they can have an edition for photographic prints sized approximately 96x48 inches and a separate edition for photographic prints sized approximately 48x24 inches. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.

Return policy

VAST photos are handmade fine art pieces created specifically for you by our expert craftspeople in New York City. We put every effort into ensuring that your piece is impeccably made to the highest standard.

We pride ourselves on creating truly one-of-a-kind photographs that our clients fall in love with. However, if you are unsatisfied with your VAST photo for any reason, simply let us know within 14 days of the photo being delivered. If your dissatisfaction is due to a product defect, we will ship the photo back to our facility at our expense and send you a replacement photo. If your dissatisfaction is due to any other reason, we will ship it back to our facility and issue you a refund of your purchase amount less the shipping costs you paid when ordering and the shipping costs we incurred to facilitate the return.
